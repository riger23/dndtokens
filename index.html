Dungeons and Dragons Token Generator

How to use this tool

Find an image of the monster from which you would want to create tokens and copy the image URL into the "Image URL" field.

Then choose how many tokens you need and what size they should be. Size "medium" means a medium creature in the Dungeons and Dragons Universe and results in a 2.5cm wide printed token. Size "large" means a large creature, being 5cm wide when printed.

Generate the tokens by clicking "Add Token". After that, you can add tokens from another image or print the tokens by clicking "Print Tokens".

You can reset the tool by clicking "Clear All", which will remove all tokens too.

For saving your work, you can download a text-file with all information needed to your computer and re-import that later to continue.
